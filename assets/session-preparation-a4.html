Preparation sheet before your session

Belén CarpioCuenca + Zoom · English / Spanish

Bring this filled in or simply use it to think. The goal is not to diagnose your family. The goal is to arrive with one clear thread so the session can find the pattern faster and with more care. One theme per configuration is enough.

1. The one theme

What keeps repeating, even when you try to change it? / ¿Qué se repite aunque intentes cambiarlo?

2. Where it appears

  • Love / couple / marriage
  • Money, success, visibility
  • Belonging, migration, exile
  • Grief, silence, family secrets
  • Body tension or repeated emotional reaction
  • Work, business, projects, money flow

3. People and forces

Who may belong in the configuration?

4. What you know

  • Early deaths, separations, adoptions
  • Exclusions, migration, war, bankruptcy
  • Repeated names, roles, marriages, illnesses
  • Stories no one likes to mention

5. A respectful intention

Write one sentence that does not blame anyone and does not demand a miracle. Example: “I want to see what I am carrying that may not belong only to me.”

Before the session

  • You do not need to do a lot before a configuration.
  • Choose a quiet place if you are on Zoom.
  • Have water, tissues, and a notebook nearby.
  • Avoid arriving rushed from conflict or heavy alcohol.
  • Stay in discovery around the one theme you want to work.

After the session

  • Leave 20-30 minutes without obligations if possible.
  • Let the experience process without analyzing it immediately.
  • For a few days, avoid explaining it to everyone.
  • Notice dreams, body sensations, memories, and relief.
  • Drink water and take a bath if that helps you keep releasing.
